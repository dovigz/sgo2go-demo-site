Every child deserves the right classroom.

Since 2011, Cedar Valley Scholarship Fund has connected generous neighbors with families who need a hand covering tuition. We believe a great education shouldn't depend on a zip code or a paycheck.

We work directly with our partner schools to identify families in need, verify eligibility, and award scholarships that follow the student — year after year, all the way to graduation.

Need-based awards

Scholarships are awarded on demonstrated financial need, reviewed with each partner school's financial aid office.

23 partner schools

From Lincoln High School to St. Anne's Academy, our partners span the whole valley — independent, parochial, and specialty schools.

Give with confidence

Every dollar donated is tracked from gift to classroom, with full transparency for donors and schools alike.
